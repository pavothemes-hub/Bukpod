# Archive & Category Page

**Step 1** - Go to **WordPress Dashboard > Theme Builder > Archive**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (61).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 2** - Select **Add New Header or Footer**

**Step 3 -** Name the Builder

**Step 4** - Choose the **“Type of Template”** whether you want to create a Archive.

**Step 5** - Select target locations with the **“Display On”** where the template should appear.

**Step 6** - Set **“User Roles”** for whom the template will be displayed.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (62).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 7** - Click **‘Edit with Elementor’**

**Step 8** - Drag or drop the element that you want to build your template style with a limited layout

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (2).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

After all, click **"Publish"** for settings.
