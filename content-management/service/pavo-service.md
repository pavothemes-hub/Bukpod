# Bukpod Service Widget

Use the **Bukpod Service** widget to display a grid or list of your Services on any page built with Elementor.

**Step 1** - Edit a page with Elementor, then drag the **Bukpod Service** widget onto the canvas (search "Service" in the widget panel, under the Bukpod widget category).

**Step 2** - In the **Content** tab, choose the number of services to show, the Service **Category** to filter by, and the layout/columns.

**Step 3** - Each service card links to its own single Service page — the same URL used across the archive, category, and single templates.

**Step 4** - Use the **Style** tab to match spacing, colors, and typography to the rest of your site.
