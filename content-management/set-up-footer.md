# Set up Footer

Go to **WordPress Dashboard > Theme Builder > Add New**, name the template, set **Type of Template** to **Footer**, then click **Edit with Elementor**.

By bringing the power of the Elementor editor to the header/footer areas, you can design them any way you like, full-width included, and complete projects faster than ever. Save your footer as a template and reuse it across other projects.

* Design your footer freely with any Elementor widgets — columns, the **Bukpod Nav Menu** widget for footer links, social icons, a MailChimp sign-up form, and so on.
* After you've finished designing, click **Publish**.

{% hint style="info" %}
Need a starting point? You can also add a **Before Footer** block (Type of Template: **Before Footer**) for a CTA banner or newsletter strip that sits above the footer on every page.
{% endhint %}

## How are Conditions Displayed?

Back in **Theme Builder**, edit the template's **Display On** and **User Roles** settings to choose where it should appear — for example **Entire Website**, or specific pages/post types only.

Click **Publish**, then **Save & Close**.
