# Add New Team Member

**Step 1** - Log in to your WordPress Administration Panel (Dashboard).

**Step 2** - Edit the page where your team section lives (or create a new page), then click **Edit with Elementor**.

**Step 3** - Drag the **Bukpod Team Box** widget onto the page.

**Step 4** - In the **Content** tab, find the **Team Members** repeater and click **Add Item** to add a new member.

**Step 5** - Fill in each member's name, role/title, photo, short bio, and social links.

**Step 6** - Reorder members by dragging items up or down in the repeater list, or remove one with its trash icon.

**Step 7** - Click **Update** (or **Publish**) to save.
