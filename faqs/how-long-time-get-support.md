# How long time get Support

## I. **Where to get support?**

If you are unable to find your answer here in our documentation, feel free to ask for help from our support team, all you have to do is submit a ticket via :

#### _Ticket support:_ [_https://pavothemes.com/_](https://pavothemes.com/)_​_

## **II.** **Support Policy**

Our working time is 8.00 AM - 5.30 PM, Monday to Friday (GMT+7). In some cases, the waiting time can be extended to **48 hours**. Support requests sent during weekends or public holidays will be processed next Monday or the next working day.

**Our Support System period is 6 months from the purchase date.** During those 6 months, our support team is available to provide item support services for all of the customer's issues.

**Direct Support System provided by us through the post a ticket.** We do support all the features or problems that belong to our theme. We are not able to provide support for code customization or other third-party plugins. If you need any minor customize we suggest you hire WordPress developers.

We're following Envato Support Policy, please click [here](http://themeforest.net/page/item_support_policy) for more information.

### **1. Before Submitting a ticket.**

To speed up your request ticket, please check the following information before you post a ticket.

* Make sure you are **running the latest version of the Bukpod theme** and the latest version of WordPress.
* &#x20;Always **check for updated** information.
* &#x20;Carefully read our **documentation** for the answer to your question.
* &#x20;In some cases, try to **disable any additional 3rd party plugins** to see if this fixes the issue.
* &#x20;Make sure you’ve **cleared all your caches**, browser, and website.

### **2. Extending and renewing** <a href="#b-extending-and-renewing" id="b-extending-and-renewing"></a>

To extend or renew an item’s support, you must have less than 6 months of the existing support period remaining, and also the author must still be offering support for their item.

Please follow these steps to renew the support item:

* Log in to your account.
* Move over your username and click on **"Downloads"** from the drop-down menu.
* The downloads section displays a list of all the items purchased using your account.
* Click on **"Renew support now"** or **"Extend now"** next to the item you wish to renew the item support for and that’s it.

For more information, please click [here](https://help.market.envato.com/hc/en-us/articles/207886473-Extending-and-Renewing-Item-Support).

### 3. Can’t extend or renew <a href="#c-cant-extend-or-renew" id="c-cant-extend-or-renew"></a>

There are two reasons for _this problem:_

* You have more than 6 months of support remaining on your current support grantor.
* The author has been nominated to no longer support the item.
