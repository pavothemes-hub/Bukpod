# How To Submit Ticket

If you have any problems, you can submit your problem in the ticket. We will check it and resolve it as soon as possible.‌

1. **Step 1:** Go to this link: [https://pavothemes.ticksy.com/](https://pavothemes.ticksy.com/) and then it will go to submit page.
2. **Step 2:** Click to the button "**Submit a ticket**" to display the form submit‌.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (180).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

![](https://gblobscdn.gitbook.com/assets%2F-MHy5dy5uQ67W56zMcvB%2F-MINoh7o82LmpT2qf879%2F-MINqQAtsdfkVWMEpkhp%2Fimage.png?alt=media\&token=443faece-95f4-45c5-bab6-28d7e4f46ace)

3\. **Step 3:** Please choose the category of the theme that you bought

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (181).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4\. **Step 4:** Fill in all information about: **Enter your Purchase Code**, **Registration**, ticket description, and Ticket Description then click on to button "**Submit Ticket**". Then please wait and keep contact via ticket, when we check and fix it, we will contact you via ticket.
